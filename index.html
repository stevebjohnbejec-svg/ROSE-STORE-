HISTORY OF THE BUSINESS

ROSE Store was established in 2010 by Mrs. Rose Sesaldo in Boljoon, Cebu. Starting as a small sari-sari store, it provided daily necessities to the community. Through affordable prices and friendly service, it became trusted by residents.

FEATURE LIFE STORY

Rose Sesaldo, born in 1987, built her store through patience and determination. From small beginnings with limited products, she grew the store into a trusted neighborhood shop known for kindness and honesty.

SUCCESS STORY

From a few shelves of goods, ROSE STORE became one of the most trusted stores in the area through hard work, good service, and consistency.

CHALLENGES FACED BY THE OWNER

Rose faced limited capital, budgeting struggles, supplier price increases, and competition. Despite these challenges, she remained patient and hardworking.

ROSE STORE IN 10 YEARS FROM NOW

In the future, ROSE STORE may expand into a bigger grocery-style shop offering more products and modern payment options while continuing to serve the community.

HOW THE GOVERNMENT HELPS

Government agencies like DTI and the local LGU provide seminars, business permits, and possible financial assistance to help small businesses grow and succeed.
